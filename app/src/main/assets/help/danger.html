Danger Deck

Apart from looking ugly, a main problem is that this doesn't preserve the state of the deck across restarts (like when rotating your screen). This is issue #12.

Drawing cards

There are two ways to draw a danger card:

  • The main draw button, which is what you would normally use for the first party's danger card draw.
  • The "Draw Secondary" button, which is what you would use for parties other than the first, and when you need to draw a danger card for Void Travel, the Fortune Cards item, etc.

The differences are that the "Draw Secondary" button doesn't play sound, and doesn't enable the "Map Updated" button (see below).

Undo!

If you draw a card when you're not supposed to, that's fine; you can fix it by hitting "Undo," which will put the card back on the deck. That will enable the "Shuffle Draw Pile" button, which will shuffle the remaining cards without affecting the discards.

If you screw that up, you can hit Undo again, and it will restore the draw pile to its previous order.

If you refuse to accept Fate, I suppose you could, upon drawing a card you don't like, hit Undo, hit Shuffle Draw Pile, and draw again.

Confirming map changes

If you're the kind of person who forgets to lower the danger level on the map after winning a fight, you can go into Settings and turn on "Confirm updates." Then, when you press the "Draw" button, it will be disabled until you press the "Map Updated" button. (The only point of the "Map Updated" button is to keep you from accidentally drawing a new Danger card without remembering to update post-fight danger levels.)

Sound

By default, the incomplete "Rusty" set of card readings is enabled. If you don't love the sound of Rusty's voice as much as certain people who think it's funny to refer to themselves in the third person, you can turn this off in Settings.

You can add your own recordings of you reading the cards!
